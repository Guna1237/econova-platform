# Participant Guide

## Logging In

Open the platform URL in your browser. Enter your team name and password, then click **LOGIN**. You will be asked to confirm your consent to participate and to provide your team leader's name and email before your first session.

If your account has been frozen, you will see a warning on login. You can still repay outstanding loans while frozen, but you cannot trade or bid until an administrator unfreezes your account.

---

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in you land on the main dashboard. The left sidebar shows your tabs:

| Tab | What it contains |
|---|---|
| Portfolio | Your holdings, cost basis, unrealized P&L, and cash balance |
| Market | Live asset prices and the price chart |
| Auction | The primary auction hall for new asset lots |
| Secondary Mkt | Team-listed lots available for bidding |
| Trading | Private trade offers between teams |
| Credit | Loans you have given or received |
| Treasury | T-Bill yield tracking and debt overview |
| News | Published news articles |
| Leaderboard | Ranked standings by total portfolio value |

The top bar shows your cash balance, total portfolio value, and the current simulation year and quarter.

---

## News Feed

The News tab contains articles published by the administrators. Read them carefully. Some articles describe conditions that may affect asset prices in the current or upcoming quarter. Others are about unrelated events or companies.

New articles appear without warning. Watch for notifications in the sidebar.

---

## Placing a Market Order (Order Book)

Go to the **Market** tab. Select an asset from the list on the left. The chart shows historical price data.

To place an order:
1. Choose **BUY** or **SELL**
2. Enter a quantity
3. Enter a limit price (the maximum you are willing to pay for a buy, or the minimum you will accept for a sell)
4. Click **PLACE ORDER**

Orders are matched automatically when a buy and sell order overlap in price. Matched orders execute and appear in the transaction history. Unmatched orders stay open until you cancel them.

---

## Private Trades

Go to the **Trading** tab. Here you can propose a direct trade with another team at a price you negotiate.

To send an offer:
1. Select the asset ticker
2. Choose whether you are **BUYING** or **SELLING**
3. Enter the quantity and price per unit
4. Optionally type the target team's name to send a directed offer. Leave it blank to post an open offer visible to all teams.
5. Click **SEND OFFER**

The recipient can **ACCEPT** or **REJECT** your offer. If the trade requires admin approval (you will see a notice when this mode is active), accepted offers go into an approval queue before executing.

You can also see incoming offers in the **Received** section. Accept or reject offers there.

---

## Primary Auction

Go to the **Auction** tab when the administrator announces that an auction is open.

Each auction covers one asset at a time, divided into multiple lots. The active lot will appear with a countdown. Place your bid:

1. Enter a bid amount per unit
2. Click **PLACE BID**

The highest bid when the administrator closes the lot wins. You can raise your bid at any time while the lot is open. The winner pays their bid price and receives the asset quantity in their portfolio.

If you win, the cash is deducted automatically and the holding appears in your Portfolio tab.

---

## Secondary Auction

Go to the **Secondary Mkt** tab to browse assets listed by other teams for sale.

Each listing shows the asset ticker, quantity available, the seller's reserve price (minimum acceptable bid), and the current top bid. Place a bid in the same way as the primary auction.

To list your own assets for sale in the secondary auction:
1. Go to your **Portfolio** tab
2. Click **SELL** next to a holding
3. Enter the quantity and a reserve price, then submit your listing request
4. Wait for an administrator to approve your listing. Once approved it appears in the Secondary Mkt tab for other teams to bid on.

---

## Loans and Credit

Go to the **Credit** tab to manage peer-to-peer loans.

**To offer a loan:**
1. Click **NEW LOAN OFFER**
2. Select the borrower team, enter the principal amount, interest rate (% per quarter), and any terms
3. Submit the offer

The borrower will see the offer in their Credit tab and can accept or reject it. Accepted loans go into an admin approval queue before becoming active.

**To repay a loan:**
Find the active loan in the **Loans I Owe** section and click **REPAY**. Enter the repayment amount. Interest accrues each quarter. Missing two consecutive quarterly payments will result in account freezing and the loan being marked as defaulted.

---

## Portfolio and P&L

The **Portfolio** tab shows:
- Your current holdings (quantity, average cost, current market price, market value)
- Unrealized P&L per asset (current value minus cost basis)
- Your cash balance and total debt

Realized P&L is recorded when you sell. The leaderboard ranking is based on your total portfolio value (cash + market value of holdings, minus outstanding debt).

---

## Leaderboard

The **Leaderboard** tab shows all teams ranked by net portfolio value. Use it to track your standing relative to other teams during the simulation.

---

## Tips

- The credit facility opens and closes at specific times. Watch the banner at the top of the screen for admin announcements.
- Auction lots fill quickly. If the current lot is not what you want, the next lot may offer better terms.
- Private trade offers expire or can be cancelled if not accepted. Check your **Sent** offers if you no longer want a pending offer to execute.
- Your cash balance is shown in the top bar and updates in real time. Make sure you have enough cash before placing large bids or orders.
