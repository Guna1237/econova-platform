# Writing style — applies to all UI copy, docs, news content, comments

**Banned:**
- Em dashes (—). Use commas, periods, or parentheses.
- Words: delve, leverage, robust, seamless, navigate, underscore, amid, pivotal, crucial, key (as adjective), tapestry, landscape (as metaphor), unlock, empower, foster, embark, realm, testament.
- Phrases: "It's not just X, it's Y", "in today's fast-paced world", "stands as a testament", "plays a crucial role", "navigating the complexities of".
- Three-item parallel lists for emphasis ("fast, reliable, and scalable").
- Headlines ending in questions.
- Emoji in formal copy (UI labels, news, docs).

**Prefer:**
- Short sentences. Mix with longer ones occasionally.
- Concrete nouns. "Oil prices fell 3%" not "Oil markets witnessed a notable decline".
- Active voice usually, passive when the actor genuinely doesn't matter.
- Plain verbs: use, show, fix, add, break, mean.
- Real numbers and names where you'd naturally have them.
